What is nothing?

Nothing is the absence of something, but isn't the absence of something still something? The absence of water is not nothing; it's air or some other particles right?

Where can you find nothing?

Nothing can be found everywhere, even where there is a lot of something. You just have to be able to see it. It's like what the mystics call silence. From silence comes everything.
